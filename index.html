Cyber Awareness Blogs & Resources (India Focus)

These resources address common digital and financial threats prevalent in the Indian digital landscape. Knowledge is your strongest defense!

Vishing, OTP, and KYC Scams: Protecting Your Wallet

Fraudsters often call, claiming your bank account or KYC verification is blocked, demanding your **OTP (One-Time Password)** or personal details to 'unblock' it. **Remember:** Legitimate banks and financial institutions will never call or message to ask for your OTP, PIN, or password. If in doubt, hang up and call the official bank number yourself.

Report Scams & Learn More (Cyber Crime Portal, India) →

Safe UPI and Digital Wallet Transactions

UPI scams often involve fraudsters sending a **'Request Money'** link which, if approved, debits your account instead of crediting it. **Always remember:** You only need to enter your UPI PIN when you are **sending** money, not when you are **receiving** it. Treat your UPI PIN with the same confidentiality as your ATM PIN.

NPCI Guidelines (Authoritative UPI Information) →

Securing Your Mobile and Personal Data (Aadhaar/PAN)

Your phone is the gateway to your financial life. **Ensure you always use a strong screen lock and enable device encryption.** Furthermore, be extremely cautious when sharing physical copies of sensitive documents like your **Aadhaar** or **PAN card**. Use 'Masked Aadhaar' when possible and redact the last four digits of sensitive IDs on copies.
